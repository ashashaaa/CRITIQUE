MY APP NAME

Simply put, this is a calendar app useful for people who need constant reminders, or just people in general. However this app features so much more than your regular calendar! Plan your days ahead, and review your experience in detail. There will also be periodic notifications for everything in case you forget you downloaded this app.

What is it we do?

In this reality, we are bombarded with responsiblities and huge to-do lists. This app is designed for people with memory impairments who want to record their daily activites and emotions in great detail. This is also useful as a memory aid.

Plan Ahead

There are so many things you want to do, but most of the time you forget that you even planned it. Going to the next concert, picking up your laundry. Yes, these things are important. But so is closing the windows and leaving your favourite pen in the office. Nothing is too silly to mark down. You can customise the colours of entries and colour code according the category.

Reminders

Push notifications are the perfect way to remind yourself of what's going on. Until you have confirmed on the to-do list entry that you have completed the task, the app will ask you periodically whether you have done it or not. Once you have completed a task, you have the option to write an entry of your emotions and the challenges and joys you faced while doing it.

Your Profile

You have the option to fill out your profile, and write about your relationships, again colour-coded by category and importance. This will be a great memory aid if for one moment you forget who you are.
